ReCharge Payments

“Subscription software to grow sales through recurring Shopify customers”

Started as “3 guys in Mike’s apartment”, ReCharge was using Bootstrap styling and the founders’ intuition to guide product development. I was hired to elevate the brand and bring consistency to the user experience.

DESIGN LEAD

2016–2017

USER EXPERIENCE, USER INTERFACE & IDENTITY

ReCharge’s customer acquisition strategy relied heavily on Shopify’s review system. This resulted in a short-term, user-driven feature roadmap. To keep customer service requests down, the product experience needed to be holistically debugged. The scalability of the product would improve exponentially with the refactor, but first we needed to level set our understanding of UX or risk misalignment of expectations.

Product Patterns

As the first design hire, I tackled an interface that directly reflected the structure of the database. To reduce potential strain on the back end team while improving the user flow, the front-end team and I partnered in refactoring patterns and language in the client side product experience.
