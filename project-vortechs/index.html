Project

Vortechs

Contacts App and Database

ASK

The Vortechs production team was maintaining a list of their clients in an Excel document while the sales team was using an ad hoc approach to keep track of contacts for the purpose of acquiring leads.

The teams needed to connect their data and be able to share valuable contact and production details with the rest of the organization.

RESEARCH

Through interviews with the various teams at Vortechs, I was able to extract a set of personas and supporting user stories that would help validate and solidify the business requirements. From there, I could confidently diagram a system that would deliver those requirements and could be easily expanded into a tool for the rest of the organization.

Whiteboarding

SOLUTION

A data system and workflow for Vortechs' production contacts and client details delivers admin and mobile interfaces to multiple teams from a single source of data.

Admin Portal

We recommended integrating with AirTable, a database with a spreadsheet interface, to provide Vortechs with a flexible and easy-to-use admin portal to track/maintain their contacts and productions.

Mobile App

We then designed and developed a custom mobile interface that served the contact details and production details from AirTable to both the sales team and techs in the field.
