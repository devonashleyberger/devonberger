Course Authoring

The market was new and online education was sticking to a typical structure (Lecture / Readings / Assessment) for course content. Versal’s mission was to build a community around developing “gadgets” as a means of distributing course material. Through the gadget marketplace and course creator, authors can arrange gadgets to effectively bring interaction and real time feedback to the traditionally static experience of course work.
